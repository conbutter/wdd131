How To Use GameLog

Press the help button again ([?]) to hide this help box.

How to Add a Game: Press the [+] button to the left of the help button ([?]). A form should appear. Fill out the given information, and then press the "Add Game" button at the bottom of the form to submit the content. Some of the form content is also optional, most notably the game website / store page, and an optional image to act as the box art. Once submitted, the form will close and the added game will be included at the very bottom of the page.

How to Remove a Game: Press the [X] button on the right of the cooresponding game you'd like to remove. Your browser will confirm if you'd like to delete the game, and if you select "OK", the game and all cooresponding content to it will be removed from your library log. Be careful; this action cannot be reversed! A form should appear. Fill out the given information, and then press the "Add Game" button at the bottom of the form to submit the content.

How to mark a game as a Favorite / Not Favorite:If you'd like to change a game from being a favorite or not, press the [‚ù§] button on the cooresponding game. If the button is red, it's marked as a favorite!

How to Search Your LibraryUse the search bar to the left of the help button ([?]), and type in a search term. Then, press the search button (indicated by the magnifying glass). Your library will be filtered based on your search term, whether through name, game description, or platform tag. To disable the filter, empty the search bar and hit the search button again.

Extra Details: Upon loading the page, you will notice some games were already placed as defaults to demonstrate how GameLog works with pre-existing content. Feel free to edit or remove any of this initial content to your heart's desire!

WARNING: Reloading the page will revert all of your changes. If you add or remove something, make sure to save the page content (HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT AND GAME ARRAY) to ensure you don't lose your content!
