How to Use GameLog

Get started by adding a game to your log by pressing the [+] button, and fill in the information that is relevant to you. Then, click "Add" to add it to your library! You can also edit content of any game you've added by pressing the Pencil icon, or remove them entirely by pressing [-]. Feel free to filter and search your library too by using the search bar at the top, which searches through your entire library for titles that match your query.
